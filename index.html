Appalachian Pasture Planner (APP) — an open-access tool developed under a USDA NRCS Conservation Innovation Grant (Soil Health Demonstration) to help Appalachian cattle producers increase pasture diversity through reseeding.

Recommendations draw on published Extension and USDA-NRCS research (cited with each result) and, as it becomes available, field data from experimental West Virginia pasture plantings. Always confirm with a current soil test and your local NRCS or Extension office. Inspired by the Beef Cattle Research Council's Forage U-Pick tool.
